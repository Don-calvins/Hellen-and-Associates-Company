Who We Are

Global Hellen Consultants is a professional financial services firm dedicated to helping businesses and individuals manage tax reporting, payroll obligations, accounting records, and compliance requirements efficiently.

Founded in 2015, we have grown to become one of Nairobi's most trusted financial advisory firms, serving over 500+ satisfied clients across various industries including retail, manufacturing, professional services, and non-profits.
